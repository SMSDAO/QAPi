FAQ

How does the Pro billing work?

Pro is billed at $4/month via Stripe. You'll be redirected to a secure Stripe Checkout session when you click "Upgrade to Pro". Cancel any time from your account settings — no lock-in.

Is there a free trial for Pro?

The Starter tier is free forever and gives you full access to public GitHub modules. You can upgrade to Pro at any time and your existing API key is automatically upgraded.

What counts as a "call"?

Each request to /api/resolve counts as one call. Cached responses (304 Not Modified) still count but are near-instant thanks to ETag validation.

Can I use QAPi in a CI/CD pipeline?

Yes. Set QAPI_KEY as an environment variable in your CI runner. The SDK automatically reads it and streams modules during your build — no node_modules install needed.

What is the Audited tier SLA?

Audited tier comes with a 99.99% monthly uptime SLA, dedicated infrastructure, and a named account manager. Contact sales for a custom agreement and invoicing.
